# QIDI Plus4 Mods
## Essential Mods

These are the mods I have added that I consider essential for the printer. Just be warned you'll probably void your warranty in the process:

0. [Beacon RevH](https://beacon3d.com/product/beacon-h/) - Most of the below mods are related to this - you will not regret investing in one of these.
1. [120mm Noctua Exhaust](https://www.printables.com/model/1264411-qidi-plus-4-120mm-noctua-exhaust-fan-adapter) - Worth it for the huge noise reduction it provides. I used Arctic fans as Noctua fans are pretty much impossible to find in stock with a decent delivery time here.
2. [stew675's Chamber Temperature Probe Mount](https://www.printables.com/model/1339760-qidi-plus-4-chamber-temperature-probe-mount)
3. [rtwickham's Door Hinge Seal](https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/tool/plus4-articulating-no-hands-door-hinge-seal) - I opted for this so I didn't have to print a seal in TPU.
4. [stew675's Bed Platform Levelling Blocks](https://www.printables.com/model/1212350-permanent-bed-platform-levelling-blocks-for-the-qi)
5. [Lentrave's HEPA Filter](https://www.printables.com/model/1022271-qidi-plus-4-filtration-system) - Using this filter cage with nevermore charcoal for VOC reduction.
6. [stew675's Hex Mainboard Cover and Ducting for the CPU fan](https://www.printables.com/model/1146502-qidi-plus-4-hex-mainboard-cover-for-80mm-fan-with/files) - This is an essential upgrade to reduce the noise of the printer, as well as getting the stepper motor temps down.
7. [spi0003's Poop Chute](https://www.printables.com/model/1024451-qidi-plus4-filament-poop-chute) - I split this in 2 parts and glued together to print without supports.
8. [mr.paul's Umbilical Mod](https://www.printables.com/model/1311286-qidi-plus-4-umbilical-mod-drag-chain-replacement) - I used the drag chain with clips for the beacon cable, but it kept making too much noise in the rear-right corner (and the chain sagged onto the X axis rods). This is much nicer, but a little fiddly to get set up correctly. I cut the wire loom to 40cm, and used about 8cm of heatshrink on each end. I also used hobby wire instead of the braided wire recommended, as the braided wire I got wasn't stiff enough.
9. [stew675's Super-light Rear Toolhead Cover](https://www.printables.com/model/1156164-qidi-plus4-super-light-rear-toolhead-cover). Useful for weight reduction for the toolhead.
10. [stew675's 5015 Mod](https://www.printables.com/model/1232611-modular-front-mounted-5015-blower-modification-for). I used GDSTime fans. I used his other beacon mount mod first, but I found the stock mounting point was just too small and the mount had some sag in it. This whole front-end replacement is far superior. I also replaced the hot-end fan with a GDSTime 3010, which dropped operating noise significantly.
11. [stew675's 50mm Vented Riser](https://www.printables.com/model/1336157-50mm-tall-vented-riser-for-qidi-plus4) - This uses a lot of filament, but worth it.
12. [Phaetus Conch Hotend](https://www.phaetus.com/products/conch-hotend%E2%84%A2-qidi-plus4) - Switching to these helped stop heat creep issues I was having with PETG. Note that these don't fit in the 5015 mod, but you can [print this for it instead](https://www.printables.com/model/1437244-heatsink-fan-mount-for-qidi-plus4-phaetus-conch-ho). The stock nozzle fits in the Conch mount, so you can switch hotends easily if need be.
13. [JustLine's Nebula Mod](https://www.printables.com/model/1266502-qidi-plus-4-camera-nebula) - The stock camera sucks, and can't be increased in res in conjunction with the beacon without the printer having timing panics. I took it a step further and stuck an RPi Zero onto the back of the printer and have the webcam plugged into that - I fed the webcam cable out through the riser mod above, rather than reusing the stock camera route. Separating the webcam entirely helps reduce the CPU load on the printer (I also took the WiFi dongle out for system load improvements too).
14. [stew675's Filament cutter cable clip](https://www.printables.com/model/1261260-filament-cutter-cable-clip-for-qidi-plus-4). I haven't broken any yet, but it's cheap insurance.


## Box Essential Mods

1. [GUNgrel's Box Riser](https://www.printables.com/model/1472311-qidi-box-riser-for-qidi-plus-4) - This isn't the modular box riser, I didn't want to bother with that one. This one has worked great.
2. [R985's Filament Guide](https://www.printables.com/model/1386069-qidi-box-jam-resistant-filament-guide) - Not exactly essential, but nice for keeping the PTFE tubing tidy.
3. [GoodHope's Filament Guide Bracket](https://www.printables.com/model/1408927-bowden-tube-support-qidi-plus-4-bracket-for-qidi-b) - Nice addon for the mod above.
4. [GoodHope's Box Hub Enclosure](https://www.printables.com/model/1408451-qidi-box-hub-enclosure-for-jam-resistant-filament) - For the above. Also not exactly essential, but nice.
5. [Colsifer's Spool and Hub Bracket](https://www.printables.com/model/1396483-qidi-plus-4-spool-holder-and-qidi-box-bracket) - I had a side-mount spool mod, but removed it in favour of storing the non-box printer's spools inside a cheap filament dryer.
